    Analysis

    In the table above there are several worthy data points to highlight.

    Common Qualifications:

    • Requirement of Tertiary Study, excepting significant industry and technical experience.
    • Many roles also require attainment of industry certifications or membership of related association.

    Common Sills:

    • All the group’s roles require knowledge of and technical literacy of computers.
    • Some require Programming ability while others require management and research skills.
    • All roles require communication and teamwork skills.

    Points of Difference:

    • There is a half and half split between roles that do not emphasize teamwork (software developer, software engineer, robotics engineer) and roles that require team management and relationship management (software architect, project management, network engineer).
    • While most roles do require some programming experience, the roles of Network engineer and project manager do not explicitly state its necessity (Seek 2021).

    Growth:

    • All the jobs within our job set have significantly increased projections for future workforce growth (Seek 2021 and Labour Market Information Portal 2021)
    • With a growing industry come increase wage competition, the wages in our job set are already above the national average (Y Axis 2021)


    Labour Market Information Portal (2021)

    Y Axis (2021) and Seek (2021)

    In the chart above, comparative wage figures for the financial year to May 2021 are shown. The groups ideal jobs exceed the average wage in Australia, with the ICT professional roles doing the best of our job set.

    Conclusion

    Our group has a diverse range of career aspirations. Many of the job roles contain similarities and many differences. The largest intersections in our jobs are the requirement for tertiary study with an allowable amount of industry experience as a substitute, and the need to be fluent in general computer related skills and applications as is to be expected. There is a relatively large projected job growth across most of our jobs, with salaries that are all above the national average. The highlighted jobs of our group are very different in their paths, several are management role, several are technical roles, several are engineering while others are development. Overall, our groups interests are as widely varied as the members within it.
