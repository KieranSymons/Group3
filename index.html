Personal Information

Welcome to the homepage of the IT Mob. We are a group of students taking class at RMIT Online in the Introduction to Information Technology course. This website forms part of our second assessment in this course. It is meant to demonstrate and assess our team work skills, collaboration ability, organization and researching prowess. We have thoroughly enjoyed the process of creating this website and the content within, it is our pleasure to present our ongoing learning and growth within the IT sphere. Just below here you'll find an introduction to our group members and above are the navigation options for our site
